# Online Presence

Online presence is the small player counter feature in the launcher UI. It is informational only.

---

## What It Is Good For

Think of it as a lightweight "people are around" signal, not as a precise social system.

It does not replace chat, friends, matchmaking, or account features. It just gives the launcher a small sense of activity.

---

## What It Does Not Change

If presence fails or the network is down:

- modding still works
- launching still works
- local features still work

So if the counter looks wrong, you can safely ignore it unless you are specifically debugging network-backed features.
